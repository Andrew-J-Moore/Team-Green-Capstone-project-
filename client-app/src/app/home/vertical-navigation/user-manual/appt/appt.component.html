Appointments

Creating appointments is a useful way for users to sign up for things such as advising. You can create time slots for users to sign up for, which will be displayed in both your calendar and theirs. When you first visit the appointment page, you will see a list of appointments that you have created. To view appointments that have been created for you, click Your Appointments, then Created For You in the menu. To view Your scheduled appointments, click Scheduled Appointments, then either Created By You or Created For you in the menu. that have been created for you. To view your scheduled appointments, click Scheduled Appointments at the top. Click one of these appointments to view its details.

Creating an Appointment

To create an appointment select the create appointment button at the top. A form will be shown prompting you to enter a title, description, location, and emails. A button to select groups is also shown. Clicking the select groups button will open a popup of any group available to you. Select any of the groups and the email field will be populated with the email addresses of the members of that group.

Figure 1: Appointment Creation Form

Selecting the date and times

Under the appointment information described above, a sub-form for dates and times will be shown. You click the add date button to add more dates for your appointment. Clicking the add time button on each date will add more times to that date (see Figure 2). This is not representative of the individual time slots that users will confirm. If you set 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM and enter 15 into the length field, 4 slots will be created for that date and time.

Figure 2: Date and time form

Once all appointment information is filled in the way you want, click the create button and the appointment time slots will be automatically created and sent to your calendar. Invitees will receive an email and will be able to confirm time slots.

Editing an Appointment

To edit an appointment, view its details and click the manage button. A menu will appear. Click Edit on the menu and you will be directed to a form with the Appointment information filled in. From here you can add recipients and remove ones who have not selected a time slot. You can also update the title, description and location. To confirm your changes, click the update button.

Deleting an Appointment

To delete an appointment, click delete in the manage menu. This can not be undone and will remove the appointment from your calendar, and the calendars of anyone who has selected a time slot. Make sure you are deleting the correct appointment.

Selecting a Time Slot

When viewing the details of an appointment that has been created for you, available time slots will be shown if you have not already selected one. When you click a time slot, a menu for cancel and confirm will appear. Clicking cancel will simply close the menu. Clicking confirm will select that time slot for you and automatically send it to your calendar for display.
