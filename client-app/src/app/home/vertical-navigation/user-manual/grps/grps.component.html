Groups

Groups are a useful way to organize groups of users for sharing events/calendars and creating appointments. You can also message the members of a group to communicate with them. When you first navigate to the Groups page, you will see a list of you groups. Click any of them to view the deatils including the list of members.

Creating a Group

To create a group, click the 'Create Group' button in the top left. A dropdown will appear with choices for custom or course groups. Choose the one you want to make and a form will appear.

Course Groups

Course groups are useful when you need to share things with students of a particular course, such as events or calendars. It is important to note that created a course group will allow it to be viewed by all faculty members. Only you can edit or delete the group though.

Custom Groups

Custom groups are best suited for organizing users based on some quality other than a course they are taking. For example, a group for your advisees would be useful when creating adivising appointments or a group for members of an organization would be useful in sharing events or calendars.

Creating from a file

If you have a larger number of users for which you wish to create a group, you can upload a file with their information. The system will automatically use that file to add them to your group. If the users in the file do not have accounts yet, those accounts will be created for them. Otherwise they will just be added to the group. The format of the file is the same as one you can download from a Moodle Course (see Figure 3). Both .csv and .xlsx file types are acceptable.

To finish creating your group, click the create group button. The form will disappear and the list will update.

Messaging a Group

To message a group, click the group, then click the message group button. A popup will appear with fields for the message subject and content. Fill in the information as desired and click send. The message will be sent to the email addresses of the group members. You can also send a message to a select number of group members by clicking the checkboxes next to them in the group details.

Sharing a Group

If you wish to allow other users to be able to share events, appointments, and calendars to a group you have created or to be able to message the members of your group, you can do so by clicking share under the manage menu. A popup will appear prompting you to enter the email addresses of the users you wish to share the group with. Click share and they will then have access to your group. Only you will be able to edit or delete the group though.

Editing a group

If you need to change a group, you can do so by clicking the manage button on the group details. You will be redirected to the group creation form but the group's information will be filled in. You can change whatever information you like, including adding or removing members, and click update. The form will dissappear, and the group will be updated to reflect your changes.

Deleting a Group

If you wish to delete a group, you can do so by clicking the manage button on the group details then clicking delete. The group will be deleted and removed from the list. This can not be undone so make sure you are deleting the correct group.
